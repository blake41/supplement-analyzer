Summary

The ingredients in GLP-1 Probiotic, including Akkermansia muciniphila, Clostridium butyricum, and Bifidobacterium infantis, have shown promise in supporting various aspects of health. The data collected from PubMed indicates that there is growing research supporting the claims made about the ingredients in this supplement.
